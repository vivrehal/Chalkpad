Chitkara University for Entrepreneurship Education And Development
[CEED]

Chitkara University Centre for Entreprenurship Education & Development [CEED], since its Establishment in 2014, with its arduous efforts has taken us one step closer to an Entreprenurial India. CEED is mentoring over 35 student's startups at Explore Hub, CUPB & 50+ startups are working at Chitkara Innovation Incubator Foundation(CIIF)


Entreprenurship Cell, Chitkara University (E-Cell) established under aegis of CEED, is a non-profit student's organisation dedicated to promoting the spirit of entreprenurship among student throughout India.


Since E-Cell's inception in year 2017, through the events like the StartUp Day, Mentor/Mentee Meet Up, Boot Camps Startup OPD which cover thousands of startup enthusiastic students and foster the spirit of Enterprenurship among them, core team of E-Cell is working hard tp promote the startups culture in campus and beyond.


Enteprenurship-Cell conducts a myriad of activities throughout the year, for the benefit of students with Chitkara University like >StartUP Day, Mentor-Mentee meets, Buisness Quizzes, Investor Hunt, Design Thinking Hackathones, Bootcamps, Internship Orientations, Camps Company programme etc. Register yourself now!!
